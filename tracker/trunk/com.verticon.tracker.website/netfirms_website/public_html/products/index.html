Products

The Tracker products are a suite of information management tools and network service solutions for Animal Agriculture producers, Infrastructure providers and Process control auditors. Tracker product solutions are used to

  • Document;

  • Manage;

  • Analyze;

  • Report;

  • Archive;

  • Query;

  • and Inter-exchange information between livestock business associates.

Tracker Product Line

Tracker Products:

  • Tracker Professional is a business intelligence toolkit for Data Acquisition, Analysis, Reporting and Integration;

  • Tracker Lite is an easy to use smart phone application for acquisition and retrieval of Livestock premises and animal information;

  • Tracker Cloud is a network service for archival, retrieval and query of livestock premises documents;

  • iRouter appliance is a configurable network event processor for the integration of sensors and actuators.
