Our Mission Statement:

Reduce the time, cost and effort necessary to build scalable integration solutions for service provider and large enterprise networks. 

To accomplish this mission, Verticon combines the best data mart and decision support techniques with open systems protocols and standards, including Java and distributed object based component frameworks.  These techniques enable our customers and clients to meet rapid application development deadlines, reduce data delivery costs, and implement projects using a scalable architecture. We strive to maximize resource efficiencies to keep costs in line during development, through deployment and into production state.

As part of the mission, we are building products to provide and tools to create decision support solutions (OLAP, scheduled reporting, network visualization, etc.) that address the long neglected needs of network engineers, analysts, modelers, executive managers, and their customers.  Planners, designers, engineers, network managers and other knowledge workers will obtain improved efficiency and productivity in their day to day tasks.
