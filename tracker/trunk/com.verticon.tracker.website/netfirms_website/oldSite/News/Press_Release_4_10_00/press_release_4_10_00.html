VERTICON ANNOUNCES BETA RELEASE OF TRENDEXPLORER PRODUCT.

ATLANTA, GA, April 10, 2000 – Verticon, Inc., announces beta test of its first product, TRENDexplorer.  The TRENDexplorer product is a no charge, value added application for DeskTalk Systems Inc's, TREND™ product.

Verticon President John Conlon stated, "Sophisticated users and administrators in the TREND customer base are interested in tools which help them use TREND more effectively, and offer knowledge of the structure of the TREND performance data repository.  We chose to provide the product at no charge to DeskTalk® customers in order to introduce our company and our capabilities to them.  Thanks goes to DeskTalk for providing us resources needed to develop our first offering."  Verticon expects to make another product announcement before the Spring Interop in Las Vegas.  Also look for Verticon to demonstrate the TRENDexplorer and its new product in the DeskTalk booth at the Interop, May 9, 10, and 11.

TRENDexplorer enables a user to navigate the tables and nodes in TREND repositories and visualize performance data by node.  Users can move columns and sort on a column so as to better understand the data that TREND has collected, summarized and aggregated.  TRENDexplorer is written in Java, for portability among platforms; is easy to use; and includes context sensitive help documentation.  Data sheets with more information, including technical specifications are available through Verticon's web site at www.verticon.com, as is information necessary to download the product.
