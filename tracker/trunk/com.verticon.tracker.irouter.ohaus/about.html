Introduction

The Ohaus Proxy iRouter component is a user configurable device specific proxy. It connects to Ohaus Weigh scales, reads scale information and converts the values read from the scale into iRouter measurement products.

Producer Scopes

It produces wireAdmin Measurement products that are consumed by Consumer components.
